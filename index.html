About the Set

Domain (Re): [-2, 1] Range (Im): [-1.5, 1.5] Equation: zₙ₊₁ = zₙ² + c

The coloring indicates how quickly a point escapes to infinity. Points that don't escape form the Mandelbrot set, shown in black.

Controls

  • Colormap: Changes the color scheme for the gradient.
  • Max Iterations: Increases calculation depth, revealing finer details.
  • Quality: Renders at a lower resolution for faster interaction.

History

First visualized by Benoît Mandelbrot in 1978, the set's mathematical roots are in the early 20th-century work of Pierre Fatou and Gaston Julia.
